Work

Insteo

Front End Coder

I worked here from March 2013 - November 2014.

I designed, developed, and maintained various customizable digital signs for use within their pre-existing content management system.

Here, I grew as a better programmer because we were creating different types of applications. Since they were primarily a digital signage company, I wasn't building typical looking websites but I was creating different moving and interactive sites that were customizable. The different types of things we were creating really pushed me programmatically and I honestly didn't have much prior experience besides school projects and those were mostly regular websites.

I was still pretty much a coding newbie while I worked here, but after studying at the Coding Dojo recently, I can now understandably say that I practiced AJAX calls, Normalizing CSS, the JQuery library, JQuery plugins, CSS3 animations, Javascript programming, media queries, JSON data, troubleshooting using console log, commenting and organizing my code.
